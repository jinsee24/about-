About Us

Welcome to The Table

At The Table, we blend tradition with creativity to offer you a dining experience like no other. Located in the heart of Delhi, our restaurant is a cozy retreat where fresh, local ingredients take center stage.

Our Story

Founded by Sayna Singh over a decade ago, The Table began as a small family-run eatery with a passion for simple, honest food. Today, we continue to serve our community with a commitment to farm-to-table dining and innovative dishes that honor our roots.

Meet Chef Emma

Our Executive Chef, Emma Thompson, brings over 20 years of culinary expertise to our kitchen. Her passion for rustic cuisine and dedication to local ingredients make every dish at The Rustic Table a celebration of flavor and craftsmanship.
